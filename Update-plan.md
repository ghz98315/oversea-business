# PearlGate V1 Development & Strategy Requirement Document

## Project Positioning

PearlGate is not a traditional trading company website or supplier directory.

PearlGate is positioned as:

### A China EV Charging Supply Chain Solution Platform

Focused on:

* EV charging ecosystem
* Supplier sourcing
* Factory verification
* OEM coordination
* China manufacturing insights
* Supply chain intelligence

The platform should help overseas buyers:

* Reduce sourcing risks
* Understand China manufacturing
* Find reliable EV charging suppliers
* Access verified manufacturing partners
